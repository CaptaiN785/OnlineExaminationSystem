General instructions:
  1. Make sure you appear for the online exam sitting alone in a well lit room with no background noise during the entire process of the exam.

  2. For subjects that requires rough work, make sure you do the rough works on blank sheet and not on any notebook.

  3. Make sure your device (mobile/tab/laptop/desktop) is ready and at your disposal for appearing for the online exam

  4. Make sure you have a good and stable internet connection.

  5. Make sure you DO NOT open any other link/tab on your browser apart from the exam link during the online exam.

  6. Keep an eye on the TIMER CLOCK on bottom left of the online exam page of the browser to keep a track of the time left.

  7. Do not resize or minimise the browser during the online exam.

  8. Do not close the browser during the test before your exam is complete.

  9. Once submitted, a message shall be displayed “Your Exam has been submitted successfully’ and you can logout from the student online portal.
